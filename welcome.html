Systematized OSAS Transactions System

Guidelines to Follow

  • Accomplish the application
  • Letter of application stating the purpose of accreditation of organization filed at the Office of Student Affairs and Services
  • Recommendation from the SSC President as to the acceptance of the application, organization, registration, and accreditation
  • List of officers and their respective positions and profile.
  • Letter of invitation to chosen faculty adviser.
  • Faculty adviser's letter of acceptance of responsibility addressed to the University President through the Office of Student Affairs and Services.
  • Proposed activities and project for one (1) year (Action Plan).
  • Constitution and By-laws (include Anti-Hazing).
  • For Fraternity and Sorority, Parent's Consent is required.
  • Documents should be submitted in four copies (one for University President, one for the OSAS, one for SSC, and one for file copy).
